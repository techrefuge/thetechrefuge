About Tech Refuge

Learn more about our mission to bridge the digital divide

Our Story

Tech Refuge was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to bridge the digital divide and ensure that technology is accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances.

In today's increasingly connected world, access to technology and digital literacy are not just luxuries—they are necessities for education, employment, and full participation in society. Yet millions of people around the world lack this access, creating a gap that widens existing inequalities.

Our organization works to address this challenge by providing refurbished computers, internet connectivity, and digital skills training to underserved communities. We believe that by empowering individuals with technology, we can help create pathways to opportunity and build more inclusive communities.

What We Do

Technology Access

We collect, refurbish, and distribute computers and other devices to individuals and organizations in need.

Commitment

We are committed to helping out underprivileged people in the local community.

Digital Literacy

We offer camps to help people develop essential digital skills.

Community Support

We provide ongoing technical support and resources to ensure sustainable technology use.
